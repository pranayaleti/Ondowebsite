### Step 2: Share Your Project
Tell us about:
- **Project Type**: Website, web app, SaaS, mobile app, etc.
- **Goals**: What you want to achieve
- **Timeline**: When you need it completed
- **Budget**: Your budget range (if you have one)

### Step 3: Consultation Call
During the call, we'll:
- Understand your business and goals
- Discuss your project requirements
- Answer your questions
- Provide initial recommendations
- Discuss timeline and pricing
- Plan next steps

### Step 4: Proposal & Quote
After the call, you'll receive:
- **Detailed Proposal**: Project scope and approach
- **Timeline**: Expected completion dates
- **Pricing**: Transparent cost breakdown
- **Next Steps**: How to proceed

### Step 5: Project Kickoff
Once you're ready:
- Sign agreement/contract
- Make initial payment (if applicable)
- Project kickoff meeting
- Begin development
