## What Affects Pricing

Several factors influence project pricing:

1. **Project Scope**: Number of pages, features, and complexity
2. **Timeline**: Rush projects may have additional costs
3. **Customization Level**: Fully custom vs. template-based
4. **Integrations**: Third-party services, APIs, payment processors
5. **Design Requirements**: Custom design vs. standard templates
6. **Support Duration**: Length of post-launch support included

## Pricing Models

### Fixed Price Projects
Clear scope, fixed price, predictable costs. Best for well-defined projects.

### Hourly Rates
Flexible engagement for ongoing work, changes, or undefined scope. Rates vary by project complexity.

### Retainer Agreements
Monthly retainer for ongoing development, maintenance, and support. Ensures priority access and consistent work.

### Subscription Pricing
Monthly or annual subscriptions for SaaS platforms, ongoing maintenance, or managed services.
