# Ondosoft Overhaul: Strategic & Visual Findings

## Reference Site Analysis (The Real Estate Group)
- **Typography**: Bold, large, and confident. Uses a mix of serif and sans-serif for a premium feel.
- **Layout**: Clean, minimalist, with significant whitespace. Uses a grid-based approach for services.
- **Visuals**: High-quality, large-scale imagery. Subtle motion/scrolling effects (e.g., the "ELEVATED PARTNERSHIPS" text overlay).
- **CTAs**: Clear, action-oriented (e.g., "Let's talk →").
- **Tone**: Professional yet approachable, emphasizing "Elevated Partnerships".

## Strategic Repositioning (Ondosoft)
- **Core Message**: From "freelance service" to "premium engineering partner".
- **Target Audience**: Founders, CTOs, and decision-makers at funded startups and scaling SaaS companies.
- **Value Prop**: Performance, Scalability, Revenue Growth, and Product Strategy.

## Design Direction
- **Theme**: Dark mode with sharp accents (orange/white).
- **Typography**: Inter or similar modern sans-serif for body; bold, high-impact font for headings.
- **Components**:
    - Authority-focused Hero section.
    - Solutions-based Services section.
    - Measurable Case Studies.
    - Process breakdown (Discovery → Strategy → Build → Scale).
